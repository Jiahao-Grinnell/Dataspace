Part 1A: Introduction

Do you enjoy the challenge of uncovering hidden patterns and rules? Inspired by Peter Wason's research in 1960, the 2-4-8 Game challenges you to think critically and test your ability to find patterns in data.




Part 1B: Collecting Data

In the 2-4-8 Game, you will be given one sequence that follows a specific rule. You can then enter any number of your own sequences to see if they also follow the same rule. How quickly can you guess the hidden rule?


Here are some key terms that will be recorded during the game:


  • The game will ask you for a Player ID. You can use any name you like, but this will be on the internet. Use a code name that you will remember, but do not use a name that will easily identify you. Any alphanumeric name can be used.
  • If you are playing this game as a part of a course, your instructor will provide a Group ID so you will be able to compare your results with others in your class. Again, any alphanumeric name can be used.

    Part 1C: Exploring Example Data

    Did you figure out the pattern? If yes, well done! If not, think about what may have led you to come up with the incorrect pattern? Could it be that the sequence you were first provided with made you focus on some characteristics, but not others? How many sequences did you try? Did you only try patterns that you thought would work? Did you try patterns that did not work before you made your guess?
