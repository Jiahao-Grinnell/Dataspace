DATASPACE

Dataspace is a resource base that uses real-life stories, interactive visuals, and games to support comprehensive student learning in statistics ...



Created by Shonda Kuiper and Contributors







VISUALISATIONS

Interact with data visualisations to familiarize yourself with analyzing procedures

GAMES

Explore statistical topics through hands-on gaming experiences

STORIES

Use real stories to gain insight into statistics in everyday life






The resources are organized into series of modules that touch on a variety of concepts in statistics. They employ games, stories, data visualizations, and quizzes, which facilitates comprehensive learning. In order to find the modules by the statistics concept, use Modules option in the bar, and select a game. You can also access all games and stories by going to Games or Stories.
